About

Alberto
CGArt

I'm a hard surface 3D artist focused on creating sci-fi props, weapons, and environments in Blender and Plasticity. My work spans personal projects and professional commissions.

I started the 3DArtStuff YouTube channel because I couldn't find tutorials that showed the real, unedited process. No timelapses. No magic cuts. Just honest, real-time modeling from someone who's figured it out by doing it.

When I'm not modeling, I'm building tutorials and resources so you can get there faster than I did.
